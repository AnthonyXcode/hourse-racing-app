## Comparison: MC Top 3 Place Bet vs Trio Strategies

| Strategy | Races | Staked | Returned | P&L | ROI | Hit/Place Rate |
|----------|-------|--------|----------|-----|-----|----------------|
| **MC Top 3 Place** | **28** | **$840** | **$932.7** | **+$92.7** | **+11.0%** | **53.6% placed** |

### Key Observations

1. **MC Top 3 Place is the only profitable strategy across 3 meetings.** +$92.7 profit vs -$716 (Trio Strategy B). The simplicity of the approach тАФ $30/race flat stake, no complex pool construction тАФ eliminates staking inflation risk.

2. **Lower variance, lower ceiling.** Place bets produce consistent small returns but cap the upside. The best single-race return was $91 (18 Mar R3 where #5 NOTTHESILLYONE won at 26x тЖТ $75.5 Place). Trio's best was $572 (25 Mar R1). But Place doesn't require all 3 top finishers to align.

3. **53.6% Place strike rate validates MC ranking.** The model correctly identifies top-3 finishers more than half the time from just 3 picks per race. MC #1 placed 75.0% of the time (21/28) тАФ strong evidence the MC ranking is reliable for Place purposes even without SCMP adjustments.

4. **Profitable when тЙе2 of 3 picks place.** In 16 of 28 races (57.1%), at least 2 MC top-3 picks placed тАФ and those races averaged +$9.4/race profit. The strategy survives 1/3 races (10 occurrences) when the single placer has a decent dividend.

5. **Happy Valley outperforms.** HV produced +$115 across 18 races vs -$22 at ST across 10. Smaller HV fields (typically 11-12 runners) compress Place probabilities for MC top picks.
