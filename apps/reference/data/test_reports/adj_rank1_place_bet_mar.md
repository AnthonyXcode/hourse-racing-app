# Adj Win% #1 Place Bet Strategy — Early Mar 2026 Meetings

## Strategy

- **Selection**: Pick the **#1 horse by Adj Win%** (MC + jockey boost + SCMP adjustments) — i.e. the "banker" from Strategy A reports
- **Bet**: Place bet ($10) on that single horse each race
- **Stake per race**: $10
- **Win condition**: Horse finishes in top 3 → collect Place dividend (per $10 unit)
- **Note**: For races with ≤6 runners, Place pays top 2 only (applies to R7 1 Mar, 6-runner G1)

### Key Observations

1. **Adj Win% #1 Place rate declined from Feb (60%) to early Mar (48%).** More data confirms the adjusted ranking is unreliable for Place bets — nearly half the time the #1 pick doesn't place.

2. **Raw MC Win% #1 dramatically outperforms at 75%.** Over 3 different time periods, the simpler MC-only ranking consistently finds horses that place more often. The 27-point gap (75% vs 48%) is too large to attribute to variance.

3. **Avg Place dividend is similar ($17.3 vs $18.4).** When the Adj Win% #1 does place, the dividend is decent — the issue is purely strike rate, not value per hit.

4. **SCMP adjustments are actively harmful for Place bets.** They shift rankings toward market-aligned picks that look "smart" but add noise. The raw MC #1 — which sometimes disagrees with the market — places more often and pays better when it does.

5. **Sample size is now substantial:** 80 races on Adj Win% #1 (48+60=52.5% Place rate) vs 28 races on MC Win% #1 (75.0%). The pattern is clear even if late Mar has fewer races.

## Recommendations

- [ ] **Switch to MC Win% #1 for Place bets** — 80 races of Adj Win% data shows 52.5% Place rate and -14.9% ROI. 28 races of MC Win% shows 75.0% and +38.1%. The evidence is overwhelming.
- [ ] **Skip or reduce HV stake** — Adj Win% #1 at HV: 42.3% Place rate, -27.7% ROI across 26 races. At ST it's 57.4% and -4.9%.
- [ ] **Track "4th-place misses"** — 8 of 26 failures finished 4th. Consider whether a Place+4th (forecast/exotic) bet could capture some of this value.
- [ ] **15 Mar was an outlier** (36.4% Place, -48.2% ROI). Test whether specific conditions (track, class mix) predict bad meetings.
- [ ] **Run MC Win% #1 backtest on these same 5 meetings** to confirm whether MC #1 would have outperformed Adj Win% #1 in this period too.
