## Section 8: Learnings

### What Worked
- **Purton banker selection**: 5/6 Purton bankers placed top 3 (83.3%). The jockey boost system correctly identified Purton's impact.
- **Dominant race banker calls**: R5 (#5) and R6 (#3) bankers both held — the dominant classification was accurate for identifying the top horse.
- **R7 pool composition**: All 3 result horses were in our legs — the SCMP analysis correctly identified #6 CRIMSON FLASH (trial winner, new blinkers, gate 3).

### What Didn't Work
- **雙膽拖 strategy**: 0/3 success. The second banker failed in every dual-banker race (R2, R4, R9). At HV, the variance is too high for dual bankers.
- **MC over-reliance on HV form**: Horses with 0 HV form records but strong overall profiles were systematically underrated (#7 AURIO 0.3% → won R8, #2 ARMOR GOLDEN EAGLE 0.0% → won R2). The `--form-data all` flag would have helped.
- **+Excuses flag**: 0/9 hit rate. The flag added false confidence without generating returns.
- **Tight Mode A pools (5 horses)**: 4 races used Mode A; all missed due to pool gaps. HV consistently produces more surprises than 5-horse pools can capture.
- **MC-vs-market divergences**: When MC and market strongly disagreed, the market was right 2/3 times (R2 #7, R8 #7).

### Strategy Adjustments
- [ ] **Run MC with `--form-data all`** to include cross-venue form. This would have given #7 AURIO (R8) and #2 ARMOR GOLDEN EAGLE (R2) historical data.
- [ ] **Eliminate 雙膽拖 at Happy Valley** — revert to single banker 膽拖 for all HV races. 0/3 is decisive.
- [ ] **Expand minimum pool to 6 for HV** — Mode A (5 horses) is too tight for HV's upset frequency. Force Mode B (6) as minimum.
- [ ] **Add MC-vs-market divergence safety net**: If MC gives <5% but market odds are <8x (implying >12.5%), flag for manual review and consider forced inclusion.
- [ ] **Downweight +excuses flag** — 0% hit rate across 9 applications suggests it's adding noise. Reduce from +2% to +1% or make it conditional on other supporting evidence.
- [ ] **Never exclude a Purton mount from pool** regardless of MC rating — Purton went 7/9 top 3 tonight.
