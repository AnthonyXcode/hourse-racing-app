## Section 8: Learnings

### What Worked
- **Primary banker selection was outstanding** — 9/10 primary bankers finished top 3 (90.0%). Only R1's Purton-boosted ROBOT STAR failed. The Adj Win% ranking system is reliably identifying the best horse per race.
- **Rule 9 (market odds < 15) saved R10** — WINNING OVATION (MC 0.0%, odds 4.6) was included purely on market signal and won. This is the clearest evidence that Rule 9 is essential for bridging MC blind spots.
- **Derby (R7) pool construction was textbook** — 8-horse pool at $210 was expensive but correctly captured all 3 placers including STORMY GROVE as banker (3rd). Wide Mode B+ pool was the right call for a volatile G1 race.
- **R9 雙膽拖 was the ideal use case** — both bankers (AEROVOLANIC + AMAZING PARTNERS) delivered in a dominant race. Cost of $30 for a $42 return = capital-efficient hit.

### What Didn't Work
- **雙膽拖 failed in R5 and R6** — co-bankers #2 ALL ROUND WINNER (5th) and #3 COOL BOY (4th) both missed by one position. R6 was particularly costly as all 3 result horses were in the pool. Season rate: 雙膽拖 hit 1/4 (R9 only), miss 3/4 (R2 HV, R5, R6). The structure is too fragile outside dominant races.
- **Purton boost over-anchoring** — R1 banker ROBOT STAR was selected primarily due to +7.0 Purton boost despite MC ranking only #2. He finished 6th. R6 co-banker COOL BOY also had heavy Purton boost and finished 4th. The boost magnitude of +7.0 for banker promotion is too aggressive in competitive races.
- **MC blind spot on zero-form horses** — R2 (#6 debutant won), R8 (#13 SHAMUS STORM won, "trip mystery"), R10 (#13 WINNING OVATION won, 0 MC form). Three races were affected by horses the model couldn't price. Rule 9 caught R10 but not R2 or R8.
- **Rule 9 application was inconsistent** — applied in R10 for #13 (odds 4.6) but NOT in R8 for #6 SMART AVENUE (odds 4.4). Both should have triggered inclusion.
- **Pool gaps in 14-runner C4 races** — R1, R2, R4 all had 14 runners with 6-7 horse pools; all missed due to outsiders placing. Larger fields need wider pools.

### Strategy Adjustments
- [ ] **Restrict 雙膽拖 to Dominant-only races** — only use when top Adj Win% ≥ 35% AND both bankers Adj Place% ≥ 70%. Season data: 1/4 hit rate for 雙膽拖 is not sustainable.
- [ ] **Cap Purton boost at +4% for banker promotion** — allow full +7.0 for leg inclusion but cap at +4% when determining banker rank, to prevent over-anchoring on jockey form.
- [ ] **Systematise Rule 9 application** — every horse with win odds < 15 AND Adj Place% < 20% must be reviewed against market; if odds imply ≥ 12.5% win chance (odds ≤ 8), force inclusion.
- [ ] **Add debutant trial rule** — if a debutant has a "won trial by 3+ lengths" or Conghua/ST trial win, include with minimum Place% of 15% regardless of MC.
- [ ] **Default to Mode B+ (7 horses) for 14-runner C4 races** — Pattern B pool gaps occurred in 3/4 C4 races with 14 runners.
- [ ] **Track +excuses horses excluded from pool** — R5 #5 EIGHTY LIGHT YEARS had +excuses +draw but was excluded; it ran 2nd. Consider "borderline +excuses with favourable draw" inclusion rule.
