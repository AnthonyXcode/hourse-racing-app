## Limitations

- **Closing odds ≠ pre-race odds**: Odds shift during betting; closing odds reflect final market view, not the pre-race input the live strategy would use.
- **PlaceProb is estimated**: `placeProb = min(0.90, winProb × 2.5)` is an empirical heuristic, not a full Monte Carlo simulation.
- **3T pool dividend unavailable**: Individual race Trio dividends are used as a rough proxy. Actual 3T pool payouts are typically much larger.
- **No form analysis**: Star Form, Trouble in Running, Vet Reports, and Trackwork data are not available retroactively — SCMP adjustments cannot be applied.
- **No draw/weight/gear data**: Missing pre-race inputs that the live strategy would use.
- **Payout estimates are rough**: The ROI figure should be treated as directional only. The per-leg hit rate is the most reliable metric.
