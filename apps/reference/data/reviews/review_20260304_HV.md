## Learnings

### What Worked
1. **Pool selection was excellent**: 5/9 races had ALL 3 result horses in the pool (55.6%). Pool at 88.9% for 2+ placers. The MC model + SCMP adjustments identify the right horses.
2. **SCMP +excuses flag was the night's best signal**: Horses with TIR excuses that bounced back WON 3 races (R6, R7, R8). This adjustment (+2% Win, +3% Place) was justified.
3. **Purton Elite boost justified**: 75% frame rate across 4 rides (R2 2nd, R3 1st, R5 1st, R7 1st). Always include Purton in pool.
4. **MC value picks on mid-tier horses delivered**: R8 #9 Amazing Kid (won), R9 #6 Super Unicorn (3rd), R7 #3 California Moxie (won) — model identifies genuine value.
5. **Late market moves informed good pool changes**: R5 adding #12 Enjoy Golf (24→9.7 plunge) proved correct (3rd).

### What Didn't Work
1. **Banker dependency was catastrophic**: 5/9 bankers failed. In 3 of those (R4, R6, R8), ALL 3 result horses were legs. The 膽拖 structure cost $1,061 in combined Trio dividends.
2. **MC overrates form-based top picks as bankers**: #7 Flash Star (R4, 15 odds), #2 Sports Legend (R6, 3.0 odds, claim jockey), #1 Lucky Planet (R8, front-runner 135lb) — MC's #1 picks that couldn't deliver.
3. **Ferraris completely missed by jockey tier**: L Ferraris (8.24%) rode 2 winners (R6 #3, R9 #7). Not in Elite tier — needs promotion.
4. **Big-weight bankers underperformed**: R7 #1 Ragga Bomb barely held 3rd under 135lb, R8 #1 Lucky Planet failed under 135lb. Weight tells at HV.
5. **Narrow pool excluded near-misses**: R2 #11 Verbier (6.3x, Place shortened), R5 #1 Giant Leap (8.6x, reserve) — both just outside pool and placed.

### Strategy Adjustments
- [ ] **Dual banker or full-pool threshold**: If banker Adj Place% < 60%, use full-pool C(n,3) instead of 膽拖. Evidence: R4 (43.1%), R6 (59.5%), R8 (60.7%) — all failed.
- [ ] **Increase +excuses weight**: Current +2% Win, +3% Place was correct but possibly conservative. Three +excuses horses won. Consider +3% Win, +4% Place.
- [ ] **Add Ferraris to "Good" jockey tier**: Tonight's 2-win performance suggests he's entered the boost tier. Monitor next meeting for consistency.
- [ ] **Big-weight penalty at HV**: Apply -2% Win, -3% Place for 135lb+ horses at HV in addition to other adjustments.
- [ ] **Trust market Place odds for pool edges**: Horses with Place odds shortened >30% in late market (R2 #11, R5 #1) should be forced into pool even if MC underrates them.
