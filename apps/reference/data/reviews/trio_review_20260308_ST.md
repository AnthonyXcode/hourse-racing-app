## Learnings

### What Worked
1. **Banker top-3 rate recovered to 63.6%** — best since 19-Feb. Pool quality remained excellent: 5/11 had all 3 in pool or banker + 2 legs; 11/11 had ≥2 placers.
2. **R8 mandatory Adj Place% rule was the meeting's best call**: #7 BLAZING WIND at 34 odds included solely because MC rated him #3 with 42.4% Adj Place%. Market disagreed. He ran 3rd. This rule must stay.
3. **Pattern B (Banker Hit + Pool Gap) was dominant at 5/9 misses** — pool expansion, not banker changes, is the primary lever. Reserves with +excuses / Adj Place% ≥20% at ≤16 odds should be forced into pool.
4. **Purton + gate 1 + 50/50 blend** (R10) delivered when MC was cautious. Not all MC "overvalued" flags warrant banker demotion.
5. **Pool selection**: 100% of races had ≥2 placers in pool. Horse identification is working.

### What Didn't Work
1. **Divergence override cost $294 (R9)**: Replacing banker #6 with #5 was wrong. #6 came 3rd. Override should only downgrade (full box), never swap banker.
2. **Banker fail + all 3 in legs** (R3, R6, R9): 3 occurrences, $1,850 missed. 膽拖 structure's single-banker dependency remains a drag.
3. **Reserve exclusion too conservative**: R2 (#11 won), R4 (#2 3rd), R7 (#9 2nd, #7 3rd) — reserves/outside-pool horses placed. When reserve has Adj Place% ≥20% and odds ≤16, force into pool.
4. **Purton bankers (R3, R6) failed** on AWT/wide draw and MC-overvalued mounts. Trust MC when divergence >50%.
5. **Debutant banker (R1)**: #7 MAPOGO led but faded to 4th. Never use debutants as banker.

### Strategy Adjustments
- [ ] **Divergence override: ONLY downgrade, NEVER replace banker** — Use full-pool C(n,3) box when MC disagrees >50%, but keep the original banker in the pool. Do not switch to an alternative horse as banker.
- [ ] **Expand pool for reserves with Adj Place% ≥20% and odds ≤16**: R2, R4, R7 would have qualified. Add as 7th/8th horse.
- [ ] **When MC strongly disagrees with banker (>50%), use full-pool box** for R3/R6-type scenarios. Extra cost ~$100–200 but captures "all legs" dividends.
- [ ] **Never use debutants as banker**: Minimum 2 race starts.
- [ ] **Cap Purton premium when MC rates mount outside top 3**: Don't elevate to banker based on jockey alone.
- [ ] **Trust mandatory Adj Place% ≥20% rule**: R8 #7 BLAZING WIND validated this. Keep it.
